# L68 — M365 Workforce Packaging v1

## Lemma

The workforce may be treated as deterministically packaged only when: (1) the packaging contract exists with four layers and five governance rules, (2) product tiers trace to certification evidence, (3) persona classes match the certified registry partition, and (4) no claim exceeds the release gate evidence.

## Proof Sketch

1. If four packaging layers exist with outputs and failure modes, packaging is structured.
2. If core tier matches 4 registry-backed personas and expansion matches 35 contract-only, partition holds.
3. If product boundary traces to release gate, claims are bounded.
4. Therefore packaging is deterministic and bounded.
