# L71 — M365 Support Boundary v2

## Lemma

Support may be treated as deterministically bounded only when: (1) the support contract exists with four layers and five governance rules, (2) three support tiers are defined, (3) escalation paths are deterministic, and (4) customer sovereignty is explicit.

## Proof Sketch

1. If four support layers exist, the model is structured.
2. If three tiers are defined, support scope is clear.
3. If escalation paths are deterministic, incident response is predictable.
4. Therefore support is deterministic and bounded.
