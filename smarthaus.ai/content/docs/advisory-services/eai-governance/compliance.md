# Compliance Mappings

## Regulatory Framework Integration

AIDF provides mathematical mappings to major regulatory frameworks, ensuring compliance through proof rather than documentation.

3. **Robustness Control**
   - Formal validation of system resilience

## Implementation Approach

### 1. Compliance Assessment
- **Gap Analysis**: Identify compliance requirements not covered by current controls
- **Risk Evaluation**: Assess compliance risks and impact
- **Control Mapping**: Map existing controls to compliance requirements

### 2. Control Enhancement
- **Proof Construction**: Build mathematical proofs for missing compliance requirements
- **Control Implementation**: Deploy enhanced governance controls
- **Validation Testing**: Verify control effectiveness

### 3. Continuous Monitoring
- **Runtime Validation**: Continuous verification of compliance controls
- **Audit Preparation**: Automated generation of compliance evidence
- **Compliance Reporting**: Regular reporting on compliance status
